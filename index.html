Introduction

My name is Zachary Thompson and I am from Albuquerque, New Mexico. I have a Bachelor's Degree of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a Master's of Business Administration from the University of New Mexico. I first learned how to code using MATLAB in college to solve mechanical engineering problems. Before I graduated, I became interested in furthering my programming knowledge but was confused about what path I should choose to do so. One day I landed on the webpage freeCodeCamp.com and decided that their Front-End Web Development course was a perfect fit for me. After learning and advancing in this course for some time, I have fallen in love with the field of web development and want to pursue a career in it.

Thanks to freeCodeCamp.com and other resources, I have since become proficient in using HTML5 and CSS3, while continuing to advance my knowledge and expertise in JavaScript. My goal is to continually advance my skills while searching for employment as a Front-End Web Developer. I created this portfolio web page from scratch (using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap) to showcase my skills and accomplishments. Thank you for taking the time to explore my work and I hope that you find it interesting!
